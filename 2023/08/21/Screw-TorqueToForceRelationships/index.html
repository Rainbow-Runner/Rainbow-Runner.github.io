Screw-Torque To Force Relationships

Created2023-08-21|Updated2023-08-23

Power Screws - Torque to Force Relationships in Just Over 10 Minutes! - YouTube

Power screws are elements that are used to change angular motion to linear motion while transmitting power or developing large forces. The basis of power screws is the same for lead screws or ball screws and also for all threaded fasteners including bolts, nuts, cap screws, set screws etc, so the applications of what we will study in the following articles.

动力螺杆是用于在传递动力或产生大力的同时将角运动转变为线性运动的元件。 动力丝杠的基础与丝杠或滚珠丝杠以及所有螺纹紧固件（包括螺栓、螺母、带帽螺钉、紧定螺钉等）相同，因此我们将在以下文章中研究其应用。

Looking at a screw with sharp v threads, we can easily identify some of the terms that we will be frequently referring to. The pitch is defined as the distance between adjacent crests in the direction of the axis of the screw. The major diameter d is the largest diameter of the screw thread from crest to crest. The minor or root diameter dr is the smallest diameter or the diameter of the stem. The pitch diameter dp is a theoretical diameter between the two and it’s probably the most important one used for many of the expressions we will derive. And the thread angle is usually referred to as two alpha. The lead l is the distance a knot would move parallel to the axis of the screw when the nut is given one turn.

观察带有尖 V 形螺纹的螺钉，我们可以轻松识别一些我们经常提到的术语。 螺距定义为沿螺钉轴线方向相邻牙顶之间的距离。 大径 d 是螺纹从牙顶到牙顶的最大直径。 小直径或根直径 dr 是最小直径或茎的直径。 节圆直径 dp 是两者之间的理论直径，它可能是我们将导出的许多表达式中使用的最重要的一个。 而螺纹角通常称为二α。 导程 l 是当螺母旋转一圈时，结平行于螺钉轴线移动的距离。

For a single thread screw, the lead would be the same as the pitch as given one turn to the nut would make it advance from one crest to the next. But for multiple thread screws which you can think of as multiple strings around the cylinder each one being a thread, the lead would be equal to the pitch times the number of threads as given one turn to the nut would make it advance that many crests. The sharp v thread seen in this drawing is only used to more easily explain the terminology of threads.

对于单螺纹螺钉，导程与螺距相同，因为螺母旋转一圈将使其从一个牙顶前进到下一个牙顶。 但是对于多螺纹螺钉，您可以将其视为围绕圆柱体的多根绳子，每根绳子都是一个螺纹，导程将等于螺距乘以螺纹数，因为螺母旋转一圈将使其前进许多牙顶

During the forming operation, the crests and roots are usually flattened or rounded with several dimensions following sepecific rules depending on the profile m mj un unr.

在成型操作过程中，顶部和底部通常根据轮廓按照特定规则进行多种尺寸的压平或圆形处理。 到目前为止，螺纹牙型主要取决于应用，例如方螺纹和梯形螺纹是当您想要传输动力时用于螺钉的牙型。

Thread profiles mostly depend on the application so far example square and acme threads are profiles that are used for screws when you want to transmit power. The square threads are half a pitch tall and even through modifications are common, the acme threads also flow that rule with a 29 degree angle between crests usually referred to as 2 alpha.

As mentioned at the beginning of this article, power screws are used to transform angular motion into linear motion. For example as the screw itself rotates in place without translation in the direction of the screw axis, the nut element around it will move up or down. Worth noting here are three thing. screws are manufactured specifically to follow the right hand rule unless otherwise noted. Left hand threads are still really important for some applications. The nut element can be any threaded component that exists in angular motion with respect to the screw. So it’s not actually a knot that you would think of when talking about nuts and bolts for example. And finally the phrase in angular motion with respect to the screw means that the knot can rotate around the stationary screw moving along the screw axis, a non-rotating nut moves along the axis of a rotating screw or a non-rotating nut is static as a rotating screw moves through it as rotates. Regardless of the right slash, left hand threads, the shape of the nut element or the relative rotation of the nut and the screw what’re interested in is finding an expression that can relate the torque going into the system and resulting output force.

正如本文开头提到的，动力螺杆用于将角运动转换为直线运动。 例如，当螺杆本身旋转到位而不沿螺杆轴线方向平移时，其周围的螺母元件将向上或向下移动。 这里值得注意的是三件事。 除非另有说明，螺钉是专门按照右手定则制造的。 左手螺纹对于某些应用仍然非常重要。 螺母元件可以是相对于螺杆以角运动存在的任何螺纹部件。 因此，当您谈论螺母和螺栓时，它实际上并不是您会想到的结。最后，相对于螺杆的角运动一词意味着结可以围绕沿螺杆轴线移动的固定螺杆旋转，非旋转螺母沿旋转螺杆的轴线移动或非旋转螺母是静态的，如下所示 旋转螺杆在旋转时穿过其中。无论右斜线、左旋螺纹、螺母元件的形状或螺母和螺杆的相对旋转如何，我们感兴趣的是找到一个可以将进入系统的扭矩与产生的输出力联系起来的表达式。
