The Expressions To Relate Torques and Forces within Power Screws

Power screws are elements that are used to change angular motion to linear motion while transmitting power or developing large forces. The basis of power screws is the same for lead screws or ball screws and also for all threaded fasteners including bolts, nuts, cap screws, set screws etc, so the applications of what we will study in the following articles.

动力螺杆是用于在传递动力或产生大力的同时将角运动转变为线性运动的元件。 动力丝杠的基础与丝杠或滚珠丝杠以及所有螺纹紧固件（包括螺栓、螺母、带帽螺钉、紧定螺钉等）相同，因此我们将在以下文章中研究其应用。

Looking at a screw with sharp v threads, we can easily identify some of the terms that we will be frequently referring to. The pitch is defined as the distance between adjacent crests in the direction of the axis of the screw. The major diameter d is the largest diameter of the screw thread from crest to crest. The minor or root diameter dr is the smallest diameter or the diameter of the stem. The pitch diameter dp is a theoretical diameter between the two and it’s probably the most important one used for many of the expressions we will derive. And the thread angle is usually referred to as two alpha. The lead l is the distance a knot would move parallel to the axis of the screw when the nut is given one turn.

观察带有尖 V 形螺纹的螺钉，我们可以轻松识别一些我们经常提到的术语。 螺距定义为沿螺钉轴线方向相邻牙顶之间的距离。 大径 d 是螺纹从牙顶到牙顶的最大直径。 小直径或根直径 dr 是最小直径或茎的直径。 节圆直径 dp 是两者之间的理论直径，它可能是我们将导出的许多表达式中使用的最重要的一个。 而螺纹角通常称为二α。 导程 l 是当螺母旋转一圈时，结平行于螺钉轴线移动的距离。

For a single thread screw, the lead would be the same as the pitch as given one turn to the nut would make it advance from one crest to the next. But for multiple thread screws which you can think of as multiple strings around the cylinder each one being a thread, the lead would be equal to the pitch times the number of threads as given one turn to the nut would make it advance that many crests. The sharp v thread seen in this drawing is only used to more easily explain the terminology of threads.

紧固件的夹具内通常有两个以上的构件。 例如，垫圈将成为构件刚度计算的一部分，就像螺栓刚度一样。 等效构件刚度可以按串联弹簧计算。 软垫圈甚至低弹性 O 形圈通常可以被忽略，因为它们的刚度很小。 相对来说是可以忽略不计的。 当查看由紧固件夹紧的构件内的应力分布模式时，您会发现它类似于弯曲的圆锥形。 其简化版本比真实情况更加保守，是具有半顶角 alpha 的圆锥体，对于旧的且非常保守的计算，其值为 45 度，对于大多数组合，其值为 25 到 33 度。 我们将使用值 30，这是我们此处计算的合理值。 由于横截面积随着您移入构件而增加，因此轴向载荷产生的位移增量将不会与长度或距离 x 成正比。 然而，我们仍然可以将 d delta 写为 P*dx over A*E，其中 A 是 x 的函数。 任何点 x 处的面积都是环的面积，其中内半径是紧固件穿过的孔的半径，即螺栓大径或公称直径。 并且可以通过使用正切函数将三角形的每条对边与垫圈垫片或螺栓头的半径相加来计算外半径。

We can substitute it in the displacement equation and integrate with respect to x from 0 to the thickness t of the member. The result of the integral would be the displacement which means that the stiffness is the load over the displacement. With alpha equal to 30 degrees, we get a better expression for k and this is the expression you would use for each section of the member’s calculations, what this means is that it’s not one stiffness coefficient per member but for each section.

我们可以将其代入位移方程中，并针对 x 从 0 到构件的厚度 t 进行积分。 积分的结果将是位移，这意味着刚度是位移除以载荷。 当 alpha 等于 30 度时，我们得到了更好的 k 表达式，这是您将用于构件计算的每个部分的表达式，这意味着它不是每个构件的一个刚度系数，而是每个部分的一个刚度系数。

Let’s say you have two members and a washer between a bolt head and the nut. The washer is 0.095 inches and the first member is 0.5 inches thick and the other one is 3/4 of an inch. The conical profile that we would use to calculate the member stiffness Km would have what we call a frustum right in the middle of the grip. Therefore if we assume three distinct materials for the washer and two members we would have four k values in this case not three. And we would use the member stiffness expression four times. Lowercase d will be the same for all k‘s still the major or nominal diameter of the bolt but the outer diameter capital d will change for each k. For k1 and k4, capital d will be the diameter of the bolt head and the nut respectively. Let’s say 0.75 inches for both. And a simple calculation would be needed for capital d for k2 and k3. For example, 0.75 plus 2 1 on each side shorter sides of the 30 degree triangle for an x value of 0.595, which is 0.595 tangent of 30 for the capital d of k3

假设螺栓头和螺母之间有两个构件和一个垫圈。 垫圈长 0.095 英寸，第一个部件厚 0.5 英寸，另一个部件厚 3/4 英寸。 我们用来计算构件刚度 Km 的圆锥形轮廓将在夹具的中间有一个我们所说的截头体。 因此，如果我们假设垫圈采用三种不同的材料和两个构件，则在这种情况下我们将有四个 k 值，而不是三个。 我们将使用杆件刚度表达式四次。 小写 d 对于所有 k 来说都是相同的，仍然是螺栓的大直径或标称直径，但外径大写 d 对于 k1 和 k4 的每个 k 都会发生变化。 大写d 分别是螺栓头和螺母的直径。 假设两者都是 0.75 英寸。 对于 k2 和 k3 的大写d 需要进行简单的计算。

The bolt is a one half inch dash 13 unc. I would like to find a bolt that has a length that is suitable for this application and determine the bolt stiffness and the stiffness of the members. ASME PCC-1

Appendix O

O-1 Introduction

O-1.1

The intent of this Appendix is to provide guidance for the determination of an appropriate assembly bolt stress with due consideration for joint integrity. The detailed procedures provided in this Appendix are intended for flange joints for which controlled assembly methods are to used. Provisions are made for both a simple approach and for a joint component approach.

本附录旨在为确定适当的装配螺栓应力提供指导，同时适当考虑接头完整性。本附录中提供的详细程序适用于使用受控装配方法的法兰接头。对简单方法和联合组成部分方法作出了规定。

The historic use of a common single bolt stress across all flange sizes and ratings(e.g., 50 ksi or 345 MPa) can result in a gasket stress that does not provide sufficient margin to overcome the effects of creep/relaxation, pressure/external load, and thermal loading. In addition, use of this bolt stress can result in loading bolts past their yield strength, as in the case of austenitic stainless steels. For this reason, the approaches outlined in this Appendix are preferred.

在所有法兰尺寸和磅级（例如， 50ksi或345MPa）上使用普通单螺栓应力的历史可能会导致垫片应力不能提供足够的裕度来克服蠕变/松弛、压力/外部载荷或热载荷的影响。此外，这种螺栓应力的使用会导致螺栓的载荷超过其屈服强度，就像奥氏体不锈钢的情情况一样。因此，首选本附录中概述的方法。

O-1.2 Cautions 注意事项

The provisions of this Appendix consider that the ASME PCC-1 guidelines for the joint component condition(flange surface finish, bolt spacing, flange rigidity, bolt condition, etc.) are within acceptable limits.
