Screw-Torque To Force Relationships

Created2023-08-21|Updated2023-09-08
|Post View:

Power Screws - Torque to Force Relationships in Just Over 10 Minutes! - YouTube

Power screws are elements that are used to change angular motion to linear motion while transmitting power or developing large forces. The basis of power screws is the same for lead screws or ball screws and also for all threaded fasteners including bolts, nuts, cap screws, set screws etc, so the applications of what we will study in the following articles.

动力螺杆是用于在传递动力或产生大力的同时将角运动转变为线性运动的元件。 动力丝杠的基础与丝杠或滚珠丝杠以及所有螺纹紧固件（包括螺栓、螺母、带帽螺钉、紧定螺钉等）相同，因此我们将在以下文章中研究其应用。

Looking at a screw with sharp v threads, we can easily identify some of the terms that we will be frequently referring to. The pitch is defined as the distance between adjacent crests in the direction of the axis of the screw. The major diameter d is the largest diameter of the screw thread from crest to crest. The minor or root diameter dr is the smallest diameter or the diameter of the stem. The pitch diameter dp is a theoretical diameter between the two and it’s probably the most important one used for many of the expressions we will derive. And the thread angle is usually referred to as two alpha. The lead l is the distance a knot would move parallel to the axis of the screw when the nut is given one turn.

观察带有尖 V 形螺纹的螺钉，我们可以轻松识别一些我们经常提到的术语。 螺距定义为沿螺钉轴线方向相邻牙顶之间的距离。 大径 d 是螺纹从牙顶到牙顶的最大直径。 小直径或根直径 dr 是最小直径或茎的直径。 节圆直径 dp 是两者之间的理论直径，它可能是我们将导出的许多表达式中使用的最重要的一个。 而螺纹角通常称为二α。 导程 l 是当螺母旋转一圈时，结平行于螺钉轴线移动的距离。

For a single thread screw, the lead would be the same as the pitch as given one turn to the nut would make it advance from one crest to the next. But for multiple thread screws which you can think of as multiple strings around the cylinder each one being a thread, the lead would be equal to the pitch times the number of threads as given one turn to the nut would make it advance that many crests. The sharp v thread seen in this drawing is only used to more easily explain the terminology of threads.

对于单螺纹螺钉，导程与螺距相同，因为螺母旋转一圈将使其从一个牙顶前进到下一个牙顶。 但是对于多螺纹螺钉，您可以将其视为围绕圆柱体的多根绳子，每根绳子都是一个螺纹，导程将等于螺距乘以螺纹数，因为螺母旋转一圈将使其前进许多牙顶

During the forming operation, the crests and roots are usually flattened or rounded with several dimensions following sepecific rules depending on the profile m mj un unr.

在成型操作过程中，顶部和底部通常根据轮廓按照特定规则进行多种尺寸的压平或圆形处理。 到目前为止，螺纹牙型主要取决于应用，例如方螺纹和梯形螺纹是当您想要传输动力时用于螺钉的牙型。

Thread profiles mostly depend on the application so far example square and acme threads are profiles that are used for screws when you want to transmit power. The square threads are half a pitch tall and even through modifications are common, the acme threads also flow that rule with a 29 degree angle between crests usually referred to as 2 alpha.

As mentioned at the beginning of this article, power screws are used to transform angular motion into linear motion. For example as the screw itself rotates in place without translation in the direction of the screw axis, the nut element around it will move up or down. Worth noting here are three thing. screws are manufactured specifically to follow the right hand rule unless otherwise noted. Left hand threads are still really important for some applications. The nut element can be any threaded component that exists in angular motion with respect to the screw. So it’s not actually a knot that you would think of when talking about nuts and bolts for example. And finally the phrase in angular motion with respect to the screw means that the knot can rotate around the stationary screw moving along the screw axis, a non-rotating nut moves along the axis of a rotating screw or a non-rotating nut is static as a rotating screw moves through it as rotates. Regardless of the right slash, left hand threads, the shape of the nut element or the relative rotation of the nut and the screw what’re interested in is finding an expression that can relate the torque going into the system and resulting output force.

正如本文开头提到的，动力螺杆用于将角运动转换为直线运动。 例如，当螺杆本身旋转到位而不沿螺杆轴线方向平移时，其周围的螺母元件将向上或向下移动。 这里值得注意的是三件事。 除非另有说明，螺钉是专门按照右手定则制造的。 左手螺纹对于某些应用仍然非常重要。 螺母元件可以是相对于螺杆以角运动存在的任何螺纹部件。 因此，当您谈论螺母和螺栓时，它实际上并不是您会想到的结。最后，相对于螺杆的角运动一词意味着结可以围绕沿螺杆轴线移动的固定螺杆旋转，非旋转螺母沿旋转螺杆的轴线移动或非旋转螺母是静态的，如下所示 旋转螺杆在旋转时穿过其中。无论右斜线、左旋螺纹、螺母元件的形状或螺母和螺杆的相对旋转如何，我们感兴趣的是找到一个可以将进入系统的扭矩与产生的输出力联系起来的表达式。

To do this we will use a right hand thread screw that is only allowed to rotate while not translating linearly along its axis. It will be connected to nut element think of it as a larger element that is connected to a stage that allows the system to raise or lower a load. And we will impart a torque on the screw to move the stage up or down in which direction should the screw rotate to bring the nut upward. This is usually a point of confusion for most so think of the screw as a fastener. If you follow the right hand rule and you rotate the screw clockwise, the screw would go inside the nut which is upward. If the screw is not translating, this means that with a counter-clockwise rotation the nut and the stage would move upward.

为此，我们将使用右旋螺纹螺钉，该螺钉仅允许旋转，而不能沿其轴线线性平移。 它将连接到螺母元件，将其视为连接到允许系统升高或降低负载的平台的较大元件。 我们将在螺杆上施加扭矩，以向上或向下移动平台，螺杆旋转时应将螺母向上移动。 对于大多数人来说，这通常是一个困惑点，因此将螺钉视为紧固件。 如果遵循右手定则并顺时针旋转螺钉，螺钉将进入向上的螺母内部。 如果螺杆不平移，这意味着逆时针旋转螺母和平台将向上移动。

Let’s look at one full circumference of one thread we will unfold it so that the circular surface of one turn of the thread is flat on a cartesian plane where we can see the circumference of the screw. using the mean diameter,The lead,remember that by definition for one turn of the knot it advances a distance equal to the lead. And what we will define as the lead angle of the thread——lambda. The load which affects the surface of the whole thread would be represented by a vector f facing down. The normal reaction n would be as the name suggests normal to the surface. And assuming like i just mentioned that we are rotating the screw counterclockwise so that the load is move up or raised. The threads that i see on the front of this picture represented by the hypotenuse here would be moving left which means that the force that the nut exerts on the thread would be facing right and we call this load Pr because we are raising the load. Finally since there’s sliding between the nut and screw there will be a friction force in the opposite direction to the sliding of the knot on the screw where f is the friction coefficient. From a sum of forces in the y direction, we can solve for the normal force and substitute that expression in the sum of forces in the x direction to solve for Pr. Dividing by cosine of lambda in both the numerator and the denominator and realizing the tangent of lambda is l over pie*dm, we find an expression for the horizontal force Pr which happens tangentially to any point on the thread pf the screw. Therefore Pr is used to calculate the torque required to raise the load by multiplying it by the mean radius of the screw which is the mean diameter over 2. This would be the torque required to overcome thread friction and to raise the load, but we commonly refer to it as the torque required to raise a load.

让我们看一下一根螺纹的一个完整圆周，我们将其展开，使一圈螺纹的圆形表面平坦在笛卡尔平面上，在该平面上我们可以看到螺钉的圆周。 使用平均直径，即导程，请记住，根据定义，对于结的一圈，它前进的距离等于导程。 我们将定义为螺纹的导程角——lambda。 影响整个螺纹表面的负载将由面朝下的矢量 f 表示。 顾名思义，法向反应 n 是垂直于表面的。 假设像我刚才提到的那样，我们逆时针旋转螺钉，以便负载向上移动或升高。 我在这张图片前面看到的由斜边表示的螺纹将向左移动，这意味着螺母施加在螺纹上的力将面向右侧，我们将此负载称为 Pr 因为我们正在提高负载 。 最后，由于螺母和螺钉之间存在滑动，因此会产生与螺钉上的结滑动方向相反的摩擦力，其中 f 是摩擦系数。 根据 y 方向的力和，我们可以求解法向力，并将该表达式代入 x 方向的力和，以求解 Pr。 除以分子和分母中的 lambda 余弦，并实现 lambda 的正切是 l 在 pie*dm 上，我们找到水平力 Pr 的表达式，该表达式与 上任意点相切 螺钉的螺纹。 因此，Pr 用于计算增加负载所需的扭矩，方法是将其乘以螺杆的平均半径（即平均直径除以 2）。这将是克服螺纹摩擦和增加负载所需的扭矩，但是 我们通常将其称为提升负载所需的扭矩。

When rotating the screw clockwise instead of counterclockwise, which means that the stage will go down and not up. And therefore the load will be lowered not raised. The free body diagram of the thread will have Pr facing left or in this case PL. L for lowering the load. The friction again opposite to the sliding would go in the opposite direction. This will result in a similar expression with different signs. This is the torque required to overcome a part of the friction in lowering the load.

In some case where the load is large or where the friction is very low, the load will be large enough to make the screw spin without any external effort. When that happens, TL mathematically will be negative or zero. If the value for TL is positive, the screw is said to be self-locking and the torque will be required to lower the load.

All of this is assuming a square thread profile. For any thread profile that result in a slanted surface of contact between the nut and the screw like the acme profile, the force F representing the load would be the vertical component of the actual interaction force between the nut and the screw–F over cosine of alpha. This would result in a slight modification to the expression we found–with a new secant of alpha term in it which is the more general expression that we will always use including an alpha equal to zero. For square threads which is basically the previous expression we derived.

所有这些都假设为方形螺纹轮廓。 对于任何在螺母和螺杆之间形成倾斜接触表面的螺纹牙形（例如 Acme 牙形），代表载荷的力 F 将是螺母和螺杆之间实际相互作用力的垂直分量 - F 除以 alpha 的余弦。 这将导致我们发现的表达式略有修改——其中包含一个新的 alpha 正割，这是我们将始终使用的更通用的表达式，其中 alpha 等于 0。 对于方螺纹，这基本上是我们之前导出的表达式。
